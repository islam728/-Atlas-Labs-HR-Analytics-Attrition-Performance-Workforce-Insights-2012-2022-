## Project Overview
This project is a comprehensive **HR Analytics & Attrition Analysis** built using employee data from **2012 to 2022**.  
It is designed to support HR teams in **understanding employee behavior**, **reducing attrition**, and **managing company burn rate** through clear, data-driven insights.

The project was built **from scratch**, starting with raw tables, creating relationships between them, and ending with a **four-page interactive Power BI report** that allows analysis at both the **organizational** and **individual employee** level.

This is an **intermediate-level project**, created for learning, practice, and portfolio demonstration.

---

## Business Context
Employee attrition is costly. Beyond replacement expenses, it affects productivity, morale, and long-term financial stability.  
HR teams need visibility not only into *who is leaving*, but *why they leave* and *which employees may be at risk next*.

This project helps answer those questions by transforming historical HR data into actionable insights.

---

## Project Objectives
- Analyze employee attrition trends over a 10-year period
- Identify key drivers behind employee turnover
- Track performance, satisfaction, and engagement metrics
- Enable **individual employee analysis** for HR review
- Support workforce planning and burn rate control

## Key Insights (Examples)
- Attrition rates vary significantly by department and job role
- Employees with frequent overtime show higher attrition risk
- Early years at the company have the highest turnover
- Performance and satisfaction trends help explain attrition behavior
- Individual-level analysis enables proactive HR intervention

---

## Recommended Business Actions
- **Focus on Early Tenure Employees**  
  Strengthen onboarding and engagement during the first years of employment.

- **Manage Overtime Requirements**  
  Monitor and optimize overtime policies to reduce burnout-driven attrition.

- **Target High-Risk Roles**  
  Develop retention strategies for departments with consistently high turnover.

- **Leverage Individual Employee Insights**  
  Use performance and satisfaction trends to identify and support at-risk employees early.

- **Improve Workforce Planning**  
  Use attrition patterns to forecast hiring needs and control burn rate.
